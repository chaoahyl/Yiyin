## ✨ Product Philosophy

**Yiyin (Yiyin Music)** focuses on the local music playback experience, rejecting algorithmic recommendations and information interference.  
Here, your music library is truly your own.

- 🎧 **Local-First**: Zero online dependency.
- 🔒 **Privacy-Focused**: Fully offline, no data collection.
- 🚫 **Distraction-Free**: No ads, no recommendations, no social/comment systems.
- 🧘 **Immersive**: Designed for pure, focused listening.

## 🚀 Core Features

- 📂 **Smart Scanning**: Effortlessly index local music folders.
- 🏷 **Metadata Support**: High-performance parsing for ID3, FLAC, and APE tags.
- 🎤 **Immersive Lyrics**: Supports LRC files and embedded metadata.
- 🎼 **Library Management**: View by Artist, Album, or Playlist.
- 📱 **Remote Control**: Control playback via mobile devices over the local network.
- 🌗 **Adaptive UI**: Seamless switching between Light and Dark themes.

---

## 📱 Remote Control Guide

1. Ensure both devices are on the same local network (Wi-Fi).
2. Open Yiyin Player → Go to **Remote Control**.
3. Scan the QR code with your mobile phone to connect.
4. Remote actions supported: ▶ (Play/Pause), ⏮ (Previous), ⏭ (Next).

---

## 🎵 Lyrics Explanation

- **Recommended Tools**: LDDC or MusicTag.
- Supports **embedded tag lyrics** (Preferred).
- The player prioritizes embedded metadata; external `.lrc` files are optional.
